## Pain and inflammation.

Root canal treatment, also known as endodontic therapy, is the complete removal of the tooth's nerve. It is a dental procedure involving the cleaning and disinfection of the root canals. Bacteria and necrotic tissue are removed, allowing the tooth to be preserved. If a root canal is not performed, the infection can spread to the surrounding tissues, causing an abscess, and this complication can lead to more serious problems.

## The symptoms indicating that a root canal is necessary are:

- Sudden pain or pain when chewing
- Bad taste and bad breath
- Darkening of the tooth
- Fever

Root canal treatment is a safe and effective dental procedure. If you are going to undergo a root canal, there is no need to worry. In a short time, you will regain full functionality and the normal, natural appearance of your tooth.

## Types of root canal treatment.

Single-rooted root canal: performed on teeth that have only one root or canal. These are usually the incisors and the canines.

Double-rooted root canal: this dental treatment is carried out on teeth with two canals, such as the lower molars or the upper second premolars.

Multi-rooted root canal: performed on teeth with three or more canals, such as the upper molars.
