## What is dental plaque?

A large number of foods we eat produce bacteria, and these cause acids to stick to the teeth. When there is not adequate dental hygiene, these acids cause a layer of tartar to form. If a dental cleaning is not performed at the clinic, tartar can cause irritation and inflammation of the gums, as well as bleeding when cleaning the teeth and while eating.

The best way to prevent dental plaque from appearing is to brush your teeth after every meal; if this is not possible because you have eaten out, we suggest chewing sugar-free gum.

By maintaining proper dental hygiene and visiting the dentist every 6 months, you can achieve and maintain healthy teeth.
