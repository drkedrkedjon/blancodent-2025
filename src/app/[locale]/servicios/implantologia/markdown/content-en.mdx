## Dental implants.

Dental implants are the best solution for replacing lost teeth. The dental implant is shaped like a tooth root and, through a minimally invasive procedure, is placed into the patient's jawbone, replacing the root of the missing tooth. Highly biocompatible materials, such as titanium or zirconia, are used in their manufacture, allowing a bond with the bone called osseointegration.

Now, at Blancodent Dental Clinic, we have innovative technology for dental implants, where we can use biomaterial obtained from the patient's own extracted tooth to provide almost perfect osseointegration. This avoids biomaterials of animal or synthetic origin. There is nothing more precise than your own DNA!

The main reason we consider it advisable to replace a lost tooth with a dental implant, apart from aesthetics and appearance, is the loss of bone in the jaw, which, with age, can lead to serious consequences for the patient's dental and general health.

## Once the implant is placed,

The dental prosthesis is fitted, which will restore the patient's chewing, speech, and aesthetic functions. Dental prostheses can be fixed or removable.

The fixed dental prosthesis supported by dental implants can be either cemented or screwed in. The fixed prosthesis cannot be removed from the mouth and requires a greater number of dental implants (6-8) for placement.

The removable prosthesis is supported by fewer dental implants (2-4) and can be easily removed from the mouth, making daily dental hygiene easier. It is a more economical option than the fixed prosthesis.

## The implantation is very quick and simple.

In recent years, a new minimally invasive dental technique developed by experts in Germany has been used. The great innovation of this dental technique is that it allows the placement of dental implants without any incisions or sutures, thus avoiding pain, bleeding, and swelling, which are the main fears of patients with this type of dental treatment. It is a great advance for people with diabetes, smokers, the elderly, etc. By reducing trauma, the failure rate of dental implants is also reduced.

## Osseointegration.

Osseointegration is the firm, stable, and lasting bond between the dental implant and the bone. For this bond to occur, the dental implantation technique used and the patient's cooperation, especially in the first weeks, are very important. It is worth noting that most dental implants are successful, with no rejection. At Blancodent Dental Clinic, by using the patient's own genetic material (obtained from their extracted tooth), immune reactions are avoided.

## Contraindications?

There are few absolute contraindications for dental implants, such as malignant tumours, high-dose radiotherapy, specific infections, or serious illnesses, and some medications (bisphosphonates) that affect bone metabolism and alter normal healing.

## Can the dental implant and tooth be placed on the same day?

In very specific and favourable cases, dental implants and teeth can be placed on the same day. In selected cases, we place a dental implant and a provisional resin dental crown. The temporary dental crown will be replaced by the final ceramic or zirconia crown once the healing period has passed.

## Can dental implants be placed to improve my current prosthesis?

If the patient already has a dental prosthesis, comfort and function can also be improved by placing 2-4 dental implants in unstable full prostheses, achieving better anchorage without the need to change the prosthesis. Economically, an optimal result is achieved with minimal investment, thus eliminating the need to use pharmacy denture adhesives (such as COREGA).
