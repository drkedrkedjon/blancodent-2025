## What can cause bad breath?

What we eat influences the air we exhale. Some foods, like garlic, affect bad breath. Once food has been absorbed into the bloodstream, some of these substances are transferred to the lungs where they are expelled through breathing. Proper oral hygiene can temporarily reduce some of the smell only while the body eliminates the food. With a correct and natural diet, you can eliminate bad breath.

If you do not maintain proper daily oral hygiene, food debris produces bad odour, to which are added the bacteria that already produce bad odour by themselves. At Blancodent we will be happy to explain how to carry out proper and effective oral cleaning.

One of the most alarming signs of periodontal disease is the constant presence of bad odour or bad taste. Regular dental check-ups help in the detection and solution of the disease.

The disease of dry mouth (xerostomia) occurs when the flow of saliva decreases due to some medications or when breathing through the mouth.

Tobacco causes bad odour, stains the surface of the teeth and causes irritation of the gums. It is also a determining factor in the appearance of cancer in the mouth and pharynx.

It is also associated with other diseases such as: respiratory infections, chronic sinusitis, bronchitis, diabetes, etc.

## Solution?

Any disease causing bad odour can be detected during regular check-ups. Maintaining good oral hygiene and a proper diet is essential for eliminating bad breath.
