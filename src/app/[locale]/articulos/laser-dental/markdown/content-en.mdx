## About dental lasers

Lasers are increasingly used in dentistry. They offer us faster, cleaner treatments and, most importantly, are more accepted by patients. In addition, they provide a bloodless working field, immediate disinfection, and faster recovery.

A state-of-the-art diode laser with a wide range of indications is used in our Clinic.

At Blancodent we offer our patients minimally invasive treatments associated with new technologies. Within this trend, laser technology is a complementary or essential tool.

Our main objective with the use of these new tools is to offer our patients what they have always wanted: advanced dentistry without suffering and without fear.

## Laser treatment is very effective for the following procedures:

- Periodontal surgery.
- Cosmetic gum surgery.
- Post-surgical pain therapy.
- Dental whitening.
- Herpes.
- Temporomandibular joint pain.
- Tooth sensitivity.
- etc.

## These are the most common questions our patients have:

- Is it painful? No, and the good thing is that most treatments do not require any type of anaesthesia. Only occasionally is anaesthesia applied in gel form and without injections.
- The only protection needed is special filter glasses for eye protection.
- Does it make any noise? No, and unlike other devices used by dentists, the laser is characterised by the absence of noise and vibrations, with psychological advantages: The patient comes in relaxed and calm, knowing they will not feel pain.
