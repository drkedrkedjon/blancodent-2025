## Tobacco dims your smile.

Young people are the target of the industry, and tobacco products are evolving into new formats, just as addictive and harmful. The College of Dentists of Las Palmas (COELP) and the Canary Dental Foundation (FCD) warn of the risks to oral and general health posed by new tobacco (and nicotine) products, such as vapes, electronic cigarettes, heated tobacco devices (IQOS, Glo, Ploom, LIL…), shisha, nicotine pouches, and chewing tobacco or snus.
