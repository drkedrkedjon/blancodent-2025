## Are check-ups free?

Not all of them. The first check-up (for a new patient) comes with an estimate and is free, as it has always been. The semi-annual (occasional) check-ups, if you become one of our regular clients, are free. Others may not be free, but we will inform you in advance.

## Why don't you publish prices?

Because it is impossible to know the cost of a treatment BEFORE carrying out a check-up. The human body is unique and varies from person to person. For this reason, we cannot know in advance the necessary treatments, as if it were an oil change for a car. So, with the free first check-up, we would only need 10 minutes to give you the exact price.

## I've got health insurance so I don't have to pay anything, right?

Usually that's not the case, but come in and we'll check what your policy says. If you don't have to pay for part of a treatment, then that's how it will be.

## Are you real dentists or a prosthetics laboratory?

We are dentists-odontologists with German and Spanish qualifications. Experts in our medical profession. The vast majority of laboratory work is sent to experts in the field. Each to their own. You can be sure you are in good hands. We do not play with your health.

## Are you part of a big business? (Franchise)

No, we are a family business. Two "Güiris" and four "Canarias" make a living from it, along with their families. We are from Las Palmas. For this reason, you will be treated with a warm, family approach.
