## Safety and Protection Measures in Our Clinic

As we all know, we are facing a new health situation due to the current pandemic caused by the COVID-19 virus. This has understandably caused concern among all patients.

For this reason, and to reassure you, we inform you that we are complying with all recommendations regarding hygiene, disinfection, and sterilisation measures. In addition, all our staff have completed the Dental Action Plan for the COVID-19 De-escalation Period, as recommended by the General Council of Dentists of Spain.

### The most important measures to ensure the safety of our patients are as follows:

1. The Blancodent Dental Clinic team is highly protected with PPE (personal protective equipment) such as FFP2 masks, goggles, face shields, gowns, shoe covers, caps, etc.

2. Both in the waiting room and in the surgeries, we have air purifiers with HEPA filters, effective against COVID-19, thus reducing the risk of infection.

3. Before attending the appointment, all patients will receive detailed instructions by phone:
   - Checking the patient's health status. You may not attend with a companion (except for minors and disabled persons).
   - It is necessary to remove bracelets, rings, and earrings before coming (the virus remains on metal surfaces for 3-4 days).
   - Avoid bringing bags. Your mobile phone should be kept away and not used in the Clinic.
   - Upon arrival at the Clinic, you will be given a series of instructions and asked to disinfect your hands with hydroalcoholic gel, as well as to use shoe covers provided by us.
   - It is necessary to maintain a safe distance at reception and in the waiting room.

4. Payment should preferably be made by card and not in cash to reduce the risk of viral contamination.

5. All magazines and information leaflets have been removed from the waiting room, as it has been shown that the virus remains active for hours or even days on such surfaces.

6. The chairs in the waiting room have been reduced to the minimum possible, maintaining a safe distance of 2 metres. If several patients coincide and separation is not possible, they must wait outside the Clinic until called.

7. We have implemented a thorough and continuous cleaning, disinfection, and sterilisation protocol in the reception, waiting room, toilets, and clinical area.
