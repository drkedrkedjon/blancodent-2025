## How to get to the clinic

We think it's great if you come walking or cycling, especially now that the street is pedestrianised. If not, there are plenty of car parks, bus stops and taxi ranks nearby. There is a bus stop right in front of the clinic.

## Bus and taxi stops

The taxi ranks are near El Corte Inglés and, if you come by bus, you can take one of these lines: Buses that stop on Calle Juan Manuel Durán: OB, 21, 31, 3, 30. Or buses that stop on Avenida Mesa y López: OA, 17, 30, 47, 42, 43. Link to Guaguas Municipales with maps and timetables: guaguas.com.

## Nearby car parks

If you choose to come to the clinic by car, you can leave it in one of the following nearby car parks... The three car parks located in both El Corte Inglés buildings. Calle Juan Manuel Durán 20, called Arraiga. Calle Franchy Roca, located in the banking area, called "Saba". Parking in Plaza de España. Parking in Calle Barcelona.
